Why still on Blogger platform?

This question hits me again and again. Why I am yet on blogger? Should I switch to WordPress? And the answer was NO! There are few major reason because of which I am still on blogger, atleast as of now ;)

  1. The very first reason is that it is a child of technology giant Google, maybe many of you do not agree with my statement but I do strongly believe in this thing that Blogger has a great back support.
  2. Everything is yours: From changing font to changing whole theme everything under one code. This really impress me.
  3. No web hosting costs: As currently we do not have any income source, Blogger was our primary choice.
